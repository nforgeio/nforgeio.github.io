Remarks
 Note
Interesting Fact: OpenSSL and CertUtil report slightly different valid dates for the same certificate. It appears that CertUtil reports the actual dates whereas OpenSSL rounds ValidFrom down to 00:00:00 and rounds ValidUntil up to 23:59:59. I'm guessing OpenSSL is doing this to give clients that do not properly handle UTC conversions times some leaway at the beginning and end of a certficiate's life.
