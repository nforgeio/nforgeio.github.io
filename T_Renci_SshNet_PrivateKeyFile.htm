Remarks

Supports RSA and DSA private key in both OpenSSH and ssh.com format.

The following encryption algorithms are supported:

  • DES-EDE3-CBC
  • DES-EDE3-CFB
  • DES-CBC
  • AES-128-CBC
  • AES-192-CBC
  • AES-256-CBC
