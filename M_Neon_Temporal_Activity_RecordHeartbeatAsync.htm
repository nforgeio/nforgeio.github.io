Remarks

Long running activities need to send periodic heartbeats back to Temporal to prove that the activity is still alive. This can also be used by activities to implement checkpoints or record other details. This method sends a heartbeat with optional details encoded as a byte array.

 Note
The maximum allowed time period between heartbeats is specified in ActivityOptions when activities are executed and it's also possible to enable automatic heartbeats sent by the Temporal client.
